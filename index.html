About Me

I am a sophomore Music and Social Computing major at Mount St. Joseph University in Cincinnati, Ohio. This portfolio highlights my academic focus, leadership roles, athletic involvement, and musical experience.

Campus Leadership & Involvement

Campus Ambassador

As a Campus Ambassador at Mount St. Joseph University, I give campus tours and assist with events for high school students who are interested in attending MSJ. This role has strengthened my public speaking and leadership skills.

Vice President – Performing Arts Leadership Council (PALC)

I serve as the Vice President of PALC, a student organization that bridges the gap between the performing arts and the rest of campus. We organize events such as Open Mic Night and the Performing Arts Gala to bring students together through creative expression.

Mount St. Joseph Wrestling Team

I am a member of the Mount St. Joseph University Wrestling Team, competing in the 285 lb weight class. Wrestling has helped me develop discipline, resilience, and teamwork.
